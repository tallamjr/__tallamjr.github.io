About Me

I am passionate problem solver and life-long learner who loves research. I am always looking into different fields of science and technology such as Scientific Computing, Applied Mathematics and Machine Learning that can help me learn more about the world and that I can apply to my main domain of Astrophysics. It has always been my ambition to embark into a research career, and so after completing a MSci in Astrophysics, I continued to develop my skills further by completing a post-graduate masters in Computer Science. My major project looked at applying Machine Learning methods to radio interferometric image reconstruction.

My goal is now to pursue a PhD in the emerging field of Astroinformatics, which is an interdisciplinary field of study involving the combination of astrophysics, astronomy, data science and machine learning.

The tools of the trade I can't live without are git, vim, and tmux all on *NIX systems. I am a keen polyglot-programmer who lives at the command line and will find any excuse to stay there!
